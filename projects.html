Labyrinth the game (in developing)
It's online version of paper-and-pencil game Labyrinth. There you can give role of game master to our server and be equal in rights players of the game. You can make room for new game, invite your friends (and, of course, accept invitations), change settings of your room and start the game. Also you can do modifications for the room and play with it. Now it's in developing, but it must be very cool.
